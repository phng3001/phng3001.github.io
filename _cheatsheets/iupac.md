## Nucleotide

Code | Base | Origin of designation
-----|------|----------------------
A | A | Adenine
C | C | Cytosine 
G | G | Guanine
T | T | Thymine
U | U | Uracil
R | A or G | puRine
Y | C or T/U | pYrimidine
K | G or T/U | Keto group
M | A or C | aMino group
S | G or C | Strong interaction (3 H bonds)
W | A or T | Weak interaction (2 H bonds)
B | C or G or T/U | not-A, B follows A in the alphabet
D | A or G or T/U | not-C, D follows C
H | A or C or T/U | not-G, H follows G
V | A or C or G | not-T(U), V follows U
N |  | aNy base
. or - | gap
